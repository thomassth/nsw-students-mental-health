Hand Hygiene

Masks

Within wider environments where the virus is spreading,
masks should be worn by the general public in settings where it is not possible to maintain at least 1 meter from others.

Learn more

▶

Social Distancing

Maintain at least a 1-metre distance between yourself and others to reduce your risk of infection when they cough, sneeze or speak.
Maintain an even greater distance between yourself and others when indoors.
The further away, the better.
